About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB) in Dr. Ritu Aneja's Lab (Department of Nutrition Sciences).


My research combines Computational Biology, Single-Cell RNA-seq, Spatial Transcriptomics, and Epigenomics with machine learning models to decode tumor microenvironments and predict clinical outcomes in oncology.
