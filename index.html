About & Overview

I am a Computational Biologist and Postdoctoral Scholar at the University of Alabama at Birmingham (UAB). My research integrates bulk/single-cell transcriptomics, epigenomics, and spatial transcriptomics with statistical prognostic modeling and machine learning to uncover key clinical and immunological drivers in oncology.
